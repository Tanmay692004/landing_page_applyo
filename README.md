# Applyo

## Introduction

**Applyo** is an AI-powered platform designed to help students and fresh graduates streamline their job application process. From crafting personalized resumes and cover letters to applying for jobs faster with the optional Auto-Applier, Applyo puts job seekers in control. The platform integrates powerful features like ATS (Applicant Tracking System) optimization, autofill for job applications, and human-guided submission rules to ensure both speed and accuracy.

---

## Features

### 1. **AI-Powered Resume & Cover Letter Creation**

* **Personalized Drafts**: Automatically generates tailored resumes and cover letters based on your uploaded profile and job description (JD).
* **ATS Optimization**: Includes a built-in ATS checker that ensures your documents are optimized with the right keywords and formatting.
* **Impact-Driven Bullet Points**: Suggestions to rewrite bullet points in a way that highlights your achievements effectively.

### 2. **Auto-Fill & Auto-Apply (Chrome Extension)**

* **Autofill Fields**: Autofill job application fields (e.g., on LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed) using Applyo memory.
* **One-Click Apply**: Easily apply to jobs with minimal effort through the Applyo Chrome extension, with options to auto-submit under controlled conditions using the **Auto-Applier**.
* **Customizable Rules**: Set your rules for the Auto-Applier, including match percentages, ATS score thresholds, and approval before submission.

### 3. **Job Matching & Resume Optimization**

* **Job Matcher**: Compare your resume with job listings to determine match percentages and suggested edits.
* **Resume Field Suggestions**: Get suggestions to complete missing sections such as certifications or achievements, with pre-made templates.
* **Job Validity Checker**: Flag outdated or scam job listings based on community feedback and automated signals.

### 4. **Human-in-the-Loop Control**

* **Review Gates**: Approve every resume, cover letter, and application before submission, ensuring you maintain full control.
* **Live Activity Log**: Track every action taken by the Auto-Applier with detailed logs for transparency.

### 5. **Additional Features**

* **AI Resume Builder**: Use AI to build your resume from scratch based on your input.
* **Interview Prep Tools**: Generate common interview questions based on your resume or target role.
* **Job Tracker**: Organize your job applications with statuses, dates, and notes.
* **Bonus Tools**: Includes skill gap finders, interview tips, and portfolio advice.

---

## How It Works

1. **Import & Set Up**: Upload or paste your resume(s), specify targeted roles, and set preferences for tone and industry.
2. **Match & Optimize**: Copy-paste a job description or provide a job listing URL to see Applyo’s recommendations for optimization and keyword inclusion.
3. **Apply Your Way**: Use the **Auto-Fill Extension** for fast, one-click application filling or the **Auto-Applier** for automated submissions based on your preferences.

## Chrome Extension Features

* **Autofill Job Application Fields**: Automatically fill forms on major job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, etc.) with your Applyo profile.
* **Inline Apply Suggestions**: Receive helpful suggestions on how to improve your application (e.g., “Add 2 relevant keywords” or “Rewrite this sentence for better impact”).
* **Tone Tweaks & Grammar Suggestions**: Make one-click edits to improve tone or grammar for consistency across applications.
* **Visibility & Controls**: Preview autofilled data before submission, and undo or revert any changes in one click.

## Auto-Applier (Optional)

The **Auto-Applier** is designed for power users who want to automate job applications while maintaining full control. It comes with two modes:

1. **Guided Submit**: Applyo autofills job applications, but you manually click “Submit.”
2. **Cloud Auto-Applier**: Applyo’s cloud agent will automatically submit applications under the rules you set.

### Key Features of the Auto-Applier:

* **Review Gates**: Ensure applications are only submitted if they meet your specified ATS score or match percentage.
* **Application Limits**: Set daily limits, active hours, and throttle submissions to avoid spamming applications.
* **Template Whitelisting**: Choose which answer templates or responses to use in your applications to ensure consistency and accuracy.
* **Complete Transparency**: Access live logs showing every field filled, timestamped actions, and links for every application.

## FAQs

**Is the Auto-Applier required?**

* No, you can use Applyo just for writing and autofill without the Auto-Applier.

**Will it apply to jobs I don’t want?**

* Only if your rules allow it. You can also require your approval before submission.

**Does it work on all sites?**

* Applyo supports major job boards and career portals, and compatibility is growing. You can disable the extension on any unsupported sites.

**Can I store multiple resumes?**

* Yes, 3 resumes with the Free plan and unlimited resumes with the Pro plan.

**Does Applyo bypass CAPTCHAs or violate TOS?**

* No. We respect site rules and will never use scraping techniques or share your credentials without your consent.

**What about privacy?**

* Your data is secure, and we only use it to help you with job applications. We do not share your data with third parties.
